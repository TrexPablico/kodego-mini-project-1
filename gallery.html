Acrylic Nails

Acrylic nails are artificial nail enhancements made from a liquid monomer and a powder polymer mixture. Applied over natural nails, they provide strength, durability, and can be shaped and customized to individual preferences.

Press-on Nails

Press-on nails are artificial nails designed for easy application. They come in a variety of sizes, shapes, colors, and designs, offering a quick and convenient way to enhance your natural nails

Nail art

Nail art is a creative form of self-expression that involves decorating the nails with intricate designs, patterns, colors, and embellishments. It encompasses a wide range of techniques, including painting, stamping, applying decals, using nail wraps, and adding rhinestones or other decorative elements.

Soft-gel nails

Soft gel nails, also known as soak-off gel nails, are a type of nail enhancement that offers a lightweight and flexible alternative to traditional acrylic or hard gel nails. Soft gel is a more pliable material that can be easily shaped and molded onto the natural nail.
